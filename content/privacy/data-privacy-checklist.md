# Data Privacy Checklist

These quick adjustments reduce how much of your personal data is exposed online.

## Account hygiene
- Review connected apps and remove ones you no longer use.
- Turn on login alerts for new devices.
- Use unique passwords plus MFA on email and cloud storage.

## Device settings
- Disable location sharing for apps that do not need it.
- Limit ad tracking and clear old browsing data.
- Keep OS and browser updated to the latest version.
